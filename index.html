About Us

Marudhar Chetna Foundation is a public charitable trust founded in 2025 in the heart of Rajasthan's Thar Desert — Bikaner. Our mission is to uplift rural and backward communities through meaningful interventions in education, livelihood, environmental sustainability, and cultural preservation.

We believe in the power of grassroots action. From building learning opportunities for underprivileged students, to supporting desert farmers and artisans, our efforts aim to bring long-term, sustainable change to the people of this land.

The Trust is run by a small but dedicated team of individuals who care deeply about rural empowerment and heritage conservation. With your support, we strive to build a more just, educated, and sustainable future for desert communities.

Our Mission

To empower underserved communities in the Thar Desert by enabling access to education, sustainable livelihoods, environmental balance, and the preservation of cultural heritage.

Our Vision

A future where every child learns, every village thrives, and every tradition lives on — even in the remotest parts of India's desert.

Our Focus Areas

Comprehensive development programs for rural communities

Education

Improving access to quality education in rural communities through digital literacy, skill development, and educational support.

  • Digital literacy programs
  • School & teacher support
  • Remedial classes & libraries

Rural Development & Livelihood

Empowering rural communities through skill development, self-help groups, and women-led initiatives for sustainable livelihoods.

  • Skill development workshops
  • Self-help groups & micro-enterprises
  • Women-led initiatives

Environment

Protecting the fragile desert ecosystem through tree plantation, water conservation, and environmental awareness campaigns.

  • Tree plantation drives
  • Water conservation projects
  • Desert ecology awareness

Arts, Culture & Heritage

Preserving Rajasthan's rich cultural identity through folk arts, traditional crafts, and cultural documentation.

  • Folk music & dance preservation
  • Traditional craft revival
  • Cultural festivals & exhibitions
